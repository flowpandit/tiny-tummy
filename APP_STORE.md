## Description

Tiny Tummy helps parents track their baby's bowel movements with intelligence built in. Know what's normal for YOUR baby's age and feeding type — no more guessing.

**Smart Tracking, Not Just Logging**
Unlike generic baby trackers, Tiny Tummy uses age-adjusted thresholds based on the BITSS (Baby/Infant Toddler Stool Scale) to tell you when things are normal and when to pay attention. A breastfed 2-month-old going 5 days without a poop? That's perfectly normal. Tiny Tummy knows this — and will tell you.

**Key Features**
- Log poops in seconds with the quick-log button
- Track stool type (BITSS scale 1-7), color, and size
- Age-adjusted alerts when patterns fall outside normal range
- Red-flag color detection (white, red, black) with guidance
- Visual dashboard with frequency, consistency, and color trend charts
- Diet logging to spot food→poop correlations
- Generate PDF reports to share with your doctor
- Track multiple children with easy switching
- Evidence-based guidance for common concerns
- Light and dark mode

**100% Private. 100% Offline.**
All data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking, no ads. Tiny Tummy never connects to the internet. Your baby's health information is yours alone.

**Built for Anxious Parents**
We know the worry. "Is this normal?" "Should I call the doctor?" Tiny Tummy gives you reassurance when things are fine, and clear guidance when they're not. Designed to be used one-handed while holding your baby.

**Free or $2.99**
No subscriptions. No in-app purchases. No ads. Ever.
