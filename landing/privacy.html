Privacy Policy

Private by design. Calm by default.

Tiny Tummy is built to keep your family's baby logs on your device. No account, no advertising profile, and no background data harvesting.

Works offline No account No ads No analytics

At a glance

App logs stay in the app's private storage on your device.

We do not sell personal information or use it for advertising.

Last updated: May 3, 2026

What Tiny Tummy stores

If you choose to enter it, Tiny Tummy stores child profiles, dates of birth, feeding details, diaper and poop logs, sleep logs, symptoms, episodes, growth measurements, milestones, notes, preferences, reminders, and optional photos.

This information is stored locally in the app's private storage area. Tiny Tummy does not upload your logs to a Tiny Tummy server.

What we do not collect

Tiny Tummy does not require sign-up or login. The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or crash-reporting tools that send your logs to us.

We do not sell your personal information, use your baby logs for targeted ads, or share your logs with data brokers.

Photos and reminders

Photos attached to profiles or log entries are saved on your device. Local reminders, if enabled, are scheduled by your device and do not require a Tiny Tummy account.

Reports, exports, and sharing

Tiny Tummy can help create reports from your logs. When you export, save, share, or back up a report, you choose where that file goes. That destination may have its own privacy practices.

Purchases and app stores

If you buy Tiny Tummy through an app store, payment processing, receipts, refunds, and store account details are handled by that app store. Tiny Tummy only uses store purchase status to unlock paid features.

Website and support

This website is designed without advertising trackers. If you contact us by email, we receive the email address and message content you choose to send so we can reply. Your hosting provider, email provider, browser, or device may process ordinary technical information under their own policies.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual logs and child profiles in the app. Uninstalling the app may delete the app's local data from your device, subject to your device backup and app store settings.

Children's privacy

Tiny Tummy is intended for use by parents and caregivers, not by children. Please do not let a child submit support messages or contact details to us.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to privacy@tinytummy.au.
