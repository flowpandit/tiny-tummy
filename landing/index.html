FAQ

Short answers for busy parents.

Is this only a poop tracker?

No. Poop tracking is core, but Tiny Tummy also covers feeds, sleep, symptoms, growth, milestones, and reports.

Does it need an account?

No. Tiny Tummy is designed to work offline without account setup.

Is this a medical app?

No. It helps you keep clear records and prepare for appointments, but it does not replace medical advice.

Can I track more than one child?

Yes. Tiny Tummy supports multiple children and quick switching.

Is my data safe?

Your logs stay on your device. There are no ads, accounts, or data harvesting.
