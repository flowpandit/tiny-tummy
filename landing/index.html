Why Tiny Tummy exists

Because parents do not need another bloated baby app just to keep the day straight.

Tiny Tummy was inspired by a real family need: remembering what happened today without relying on memory, random notes, or a huge app trying to do everything.

The goal is still simple: give parents one calm, trustworthy place to log what matters, notice changes early, and walk into a doctor visit with something clearer than “I think it started a few days ago.”

Made for calm decision-making

The design stays soft and modern on purpose. The app should reduce stress, not add to it at 2 AM.

Built around real parent workflows

Quick actions, breastfeeding timers, caregiver notes, and symptom-linked episodes exist because that is how the day really works.

Offline means private

No accounts, no sync anxiety, no analytics baggage. Just your family’s records, kept local.

FAQ

Short answers for busy parents.

Is this only a poop tracker?

No. Poop tracking is still a core part of Tiny Tummy, but the app now also covers feeds, naps, symptoms, episodes, handoff, growth, and milestones.

Does it need an account?

No. The app is designed to work offline without creating an account or sending your data to the cloud.

Is this a medical app?

No. Tiny Tummy helps you keep records, understand patterns, and prepare for appointments. It does not replace medical advice.

Can I track more than one child?

Yes. Tiny Tummy supports multiple children, so one parent can manage the whole household.
