Why Tiny Tummy exists

Because parents do not need another subscription just to remember poop details.

Tiny Tummy was inspired by a real family need: keeping track of toddler poop patterns without relying on memory, random notes, or a huge baby app filled with unrelated features.

The goal is simple: give parents a cute, trustworthy place to log what matters, notice changes early, and walk into a doctor visit with something clearer than “I think it started a few days ago.”

Made for calm decision-making

FAQ

Short answers for busy parents.

Is this a medical app?

No. Tiny Tummy helps you keep records and notice patterns. It does not replace medical advice.

Does it need an account?

No. The app is designed to work offline without creating an account or sending your data to the cloud.

Can I track more than one child?

Yes. Tiny Tummy supports multiple children, so one parent can manage the whole household.

Will there be subscriptions later?

The current plan is a one-time purchase with future improvements included for existing customers.
